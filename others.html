Other Resources Worth Bookmarking

A hand-picked list of free external websites and tools that complement what you'll find here on StudyReady.

By Category

Free tools from around the web

Math

Desmos

Free graphing calculator and math tools for visualizing functions, geometry, and statistics.

Science

PhET Simulations

Interactive science and math simulations from the University of Colorado - great for any lab topic.

Reading

Project Gutenberg

Over 70,000 free ebooks, including nearly every classic novel you'll encounter in English class.

History

Khan Academy

Free courses across history, economics, and humanities with videos, articles, and practice problems.

Writing

Purdue OWL

The definitive free guide to MLA, APA, and Chicago citation formats plus grammar and essay tips.

Vocabulary

Vocabulary.com

Adaptive vocabulary practice that tracks what you know and focuses review where you need it most.

  • Flashcards Anki Spaced-repetition flashcard software used by medical students worldwide. Steep learning curve, massive payoff for heavy memorization subjects.
  • Productivity Forest App (web) Plant virtual trees while you focus. Gamifies the Pomodoro method and pairs well with StudyReady's timer.
  • Note-Taking Notion (free tier) Flexible workspace for notes, to-do lists, and databases. Free for personal use with a .edu email.
  • Language Duolingo Best free option for foreign language practice. Short daily sessions make consistent exposure easy to maintain.
  • Coding CS50 (Harvard, free) Widely considered the best free intro-to-programming course online. Available with or without a certificate.
  • Math Wolfram Alpha Step-by-step math solver covering algebra through calculus. Great for checking your work, not replacing it.

StudyReady doesn't endorse or control external websites. Links are included because we've found them genuinely useful - but always check that a resource fits your school's academic integrity policy before relying on it for graded work.
