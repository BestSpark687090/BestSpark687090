Study Tips

Habits that actually work

  • 1Review notes within 24 hours of class — retention drops sharply after the first day.
  • 2Test yourself instead of re-reading. Active recall is far more effective than passive review.
  • 3Space your study sessions over multiple days rather than cramming the night before.
  • 4Remove your phone from the room. Even face-down, its presence reduces focus.
  • 5Teach the material to someone else — or out loud to yourself. It exposes gaps fast.
  • 6Sleep is part of studying. Memory consolidation happens overnight, not at your desk.
