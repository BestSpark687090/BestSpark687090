Why Tnzrf?

Play is a proven study method

Active Recall

Tnzrf force you to retrieve information rather than passively re-read it - the single most effective study technique.

Low-Stakes Practice

Mistakes in a tnzr feel different than mistakes on a test. That separation lowers anxiety and encourages more attempts.

Short Loops

Most tnzrf here run 2-5 minutes. They fit in any gap - between classes, on a commute, or as a warm-up before a real session.

All tnzrf on StudyReady are free, require no account, and are designed to complement - not replace - real study sessions. Use them as warm-ups, breaks, or quick reviews.
